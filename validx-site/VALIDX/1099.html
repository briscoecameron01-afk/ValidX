Independent Contractor Agreement — Key Terms

By signing below, I confirm and agree to the following:

1. Independent Contractor Status. I am an independent contractor, not an employee of ValidX. I am responsible for my own taxes, insurance, and benefits. ValidX does not withhold taxes from my payments.

2. 1099-NEC Reporting. If I earn $600 or more in a calendar year, ValidX will file a 1099-NEC form with the IRS and provide me a copy by January 31 of the following year.

3. Tax Responsibility. I understand that I am responsible for paying all applicable federal, state, and local taxes on my earnings, including self-employment tax.

4. Payment Terms. I will be paid the agreed-upon rate for each completed experiment/task. ValidX retains 30–40% of the experiment fee as a platform fee. The remaining 60–70% is distributed to the tester network.

5. Confidentiality. I agree to keep all experiment details, business information, and test results confidential unless explicitly authorized to share.

6. Accuracy. I certify that all information provided in this form is true and accurate to the best of my knowledge.
