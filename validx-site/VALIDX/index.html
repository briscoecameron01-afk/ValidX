Like Uber — But for Market Validation.

ValidX is a two-sided marketplace. On one side, startups post experiments they need tested. On the other, college students across the country pick up those tests and provide real-world feedback.

Our testers are digital natives — the next generation of consumers and producers. Their insights are forward-looking, relevant, and fast. You decide how many testers run your experiment; each tester reaches a network of real people.
